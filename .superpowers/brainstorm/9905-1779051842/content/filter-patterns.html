B — Flat scrollable strip

One row, no grouping, scroll right to reveal L2/L3. Familiar YouTube/Instagram pattern. Groups are implied by order, not visual separation. L3 is cut off to hint at scroll.

Your total filter count (2 + 2 + 3 = 7 pills) fits without scrolling on most phones in option A. Option B needs a scroll but gains simplicity.
